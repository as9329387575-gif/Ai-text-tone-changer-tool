Formal vs casual: when to use each

Formal writing avoids contractions, uses precise vocabulary, and is common in reports, legal documents, and official emails. Casual tone mirrors everyday speech, using contractions and colloquialisms — great for social media or internal team chats. With this tool, you can toggle between them in seconds.

Persuasive & motivational tones in business

Persuasive writing highlights benefits and uses confident language; motivational adds energy and encouragement. Use them in sales emails, fundraisers, or team announcements. Our tool adjusts sentence structure and word choice to amplify your intent.
